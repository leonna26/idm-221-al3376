Welcome

We are seeing the emergence of China as one of the most powerful nations on earth. What kind of influence will China have upon the world, and how will its relationship with the West develop? A nation is not transformed simply by changes in policies, technology, or even economic welfare, but rather through individuals being transformed. In the decades ahead, the future of every nation will be affected by the future of China -- but for this impact to be a positive one, China must witness its next generation of leaders being transformed into people of vision, integrity, and altruism.

Are you ready to take part in transforming China this summer?







What is ISEC?

ISEC stands for Intercollegiate Summer English Camps. We run summer camps all over China, teaching English to students of all ages and abilities.

What's in store?

  • You and your team will spend your summer leading several English camps around China
  • You will attend orientation and debriefing events to equip you for the beginning and end of the program
  • You will make strong friendships as you live in close community with your US-UK team
  • You will build meaningful relationships with your students and local Chinese teaching assistants Chinese teaching assistants will help you with classroom activities and provide you with cultural and language support
