Schedule


A Typical ISEC Summer

Week 1

Orientation in China. Meet and get to know other ISECers, learn about how to teach English, share lesson plan ideas, gain encouragement for the summer ahead.

Week 2-4

Teach at a 7-day camp with local assistant teachers in partnership with a local school. Gave classes in the mornings, and played games with the kids (including water balloon fights!) in the afternoons.

Week 5

Debrief. Meet up with the whole team again to share stories and discuss how we can apply what we have learned in our daily lives back home.
